Upcoming Events

There are a number of events happening almost every week. Our general meetup is usually the last Thursday of the month. For up-to-date information, please check our meetup page.

Your Python Project

In addition to hosting periodic project days, we have a mailing list setup for getting local help on your problem. You can also bring a tough problem to the monthly general meeting as well.

Hire a Pythonista

Are you looking to hire a Python developer in San Diego? Post a brief description of your opening to our mailing list. Please specify whether it is freelance or full-time. Be concise and respectful.

Hosts & Sponsors

We would like to thank the companies and organizations who have hosted or sponsored past events. Let us know if you would like to become a sponsor or host.
